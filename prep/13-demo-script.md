## The 10-Minute Demo Flow

### MINUTE 0-1: Opening & Context

**What to say:**
```
"Thanks for making time. Before we dive in‚Äîcan I ask: How many calls do you think you miss in a typical week? Just ballpark."
```

**Why:** Get their number. Make it personal. Set up the ROI conversation later.

**If they don't know:**
```
"Most people don't track it. I didn't either until I realized I was losing jobs. For context, I was missing 8-10 per week."
```

**Transition:**
```
"Cool. So here's what I built. In a second, I'm going to have you call a number and pretend you're a customer with a problem. You'll experience exactly what your customers would experience."
```

---

### MINUTE 1-3: The Live Call

**Give them the number:**
```
"Call this number: [DEMO NUMBER]"

"Pretend you're a customer with a leaky faucet in your basement. Book an appointment if it makes sense. Just act natural."
```

**While they're calling:**
- Watch your dashboard/phone
- Don't talk over the experience
- Let them listen to the full interaction
- Take notes on what they booked/said

**What you should see:**
- Call connected to AI
- Text summary arriving on your phone
- Potential calendar booking

---

### MINUTE 3-4: Debrief the Experience

**Ask:**
```
"What'd you think? Talked to Ali, right?"
```

**Address their reaction:**
- If surprised by quality: "Most people can't tell it's AI on first call."
- If skeptical: "Fair. It gets better with your specific business context."
- If impressed: "Yeah, the tech has come a long way."

**Show the backend:**
```
"Let me show you what just happened behind the scenes:"
```

**Show:**
1. **Text summary** (pull out your phone)
   - "See this? I got this text 30 seconds after you called."
   - Show: Caller info, what they wanted, urgency

2. **Calendar booking** (if they booked)
   - "And if you booked, it already shows up here."
   - Show: Appointment in Cal.com

### MINUTE 6-7: ROI Conversation

**Ask:**
```
"Let's talk numbers. What's your average ticket?"
```

```
"Alright, so if you're missing say [their number] calls a week, and 20% of those would book, that's [X] jobs per month you're potentially losing. At $350, that's $[X] in revenue going elsewhere."

"This costs $297 per month. One medium job pays for the whole quarter."
```

```
"In January, my first full month: captured 18 calls that would've gone to voicemail, 8 became jobs, $3,200 in revenue I would've lost."
```

**Ask:**
```
"Does that math make sense for your situation?"
```

## Questions to Ask During Demo

### Discovery Questions
1. "How many calls do you miss per week?" (Establish pain)
2. "What's your average job value?" (Calculate ROI)
3. "What do you do with calls now?" (Point of comparison)
4. "Do you get after-hours calls?" (Show 24/7 value)
5. "Ever had emergencies you missed?" (Pain point)

### Qualifying Questions
6. "Are you the one answering phones?" (Decision maker)
7. "Are you looking to hire soon?" (Timing)
8. "What would change if you captured every call?" (Vision)

### Closing Questions
9. "Does this make sense at $297/month?" (Price check)
10. "What's stopping you from starting tomorrow?" (Objection reveal)
