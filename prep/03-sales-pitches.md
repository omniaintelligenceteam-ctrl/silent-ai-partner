## üé¨ Demo Call Power Tips

### Do:
- **Let them call first** ‚Äî hearing is believing
- **Have your phone visible** ‚Äî show the text summary popping in real-time
- **Use their actual situation** ‚Äî "So if this was Mrs. Johnson with the dripping faucet..."
- **Close on next steps** ‚Äî always end with a clear action

### Don't:
- **Don't demo for more than 2 minutes** ‚Äî they get it quickly
- **Don't overwhelm with features** ‚Äî stick to what matters to them
- **Don't get defensive about "AI"** ‚Äî let the voice quality speak for itself
- **Don't leave without next steps** ‚Äî book the start or the follow-up

### Energy Check:
- Keep it conversational, not scripted
- Let them interrupt with questions
- If they love it, move fast‚Äîget them signed up while excitement is high
- If they're lukewarm, don't push‚Äîset a follow-up
