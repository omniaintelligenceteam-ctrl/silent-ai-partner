## The Angle: "FCC Ruled Against AI Outbound Calls"

### November 2024 FCC Ruling

> **FCC Declares AI-Generated Voices in Robocalls Illegal**
> 
> Effective February 2025, the FCC ruled that **outbound AI robocalls** are subject to TCPA restrictions. This means:
> - AI cold calling is now high-risk
> - Penalties: $500-$1,500 per violation
> - Class action exposure for violators

**Source:** https://www.fcc.gov/document/fcc-makes-ai-voice-cloned-calls-illegal-0

### The Key Distinction

```
TCPA Applies To:
❌ OUTBOUND calls made with AI/automated equipment
❌ Outbound calls to cell phones without consent
❌ Robocalls initiating contact

TCPA Doesn't Apply To:
✅ INBOUND calls you receive
✅ AI answering YOUR business line
✅ Customer-initiated contact
```

## Competitors Doing Outbound = Risky ⚠️

### Competitive Landscape

| Service Type | Outbound? | Risk Level |
|:-------------|:---------:|:----------:|
| **Silent AI Partner** | ❌ No | **LOW** |
| AI Cold Calling Services | ✅ Yes | **HIGH** |
| AI Lead Gen Dialers | ✅ Yes | **HIGH** |
| AI Appointment Reminders* | ⚠️ Sometimes | **MEDIUM** |
| AI Follow-up Calls | ✅ Yes | **HIGH** |

*Appointment reminders technically can be informational, but require consent.

### Why Outbound Is Risky Now

1. **FCC explicitly ruled** AI voices in robocalls illegal (Nov 2024)
2. **State AGs** pursuing AI robocall cases
3. **Plaintiff lawyers** filing TCPA class actions
4. **Carrier blocking** increasing on AI-dialed numbers
5. **Consent requirements** — hard to prove for B2B
