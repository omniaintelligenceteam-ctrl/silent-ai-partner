# 24/7 Call Answering
**Status:** ✅ LIVE
**Tier:** Answering Service ($197)

AI answers every inbound call on the first ring. 24 hours, 365 days. No voicemail, no hold music, no "press 1 for..."

## How It Works
- Vapi receives inbound call via Twilio
- AI receptionist (Sarah) answers with business greeting
- Handles conversation naturally — sounds human
- Takes message, captures caller info

## Tech Stack
- Vapi (voice platform)
- Anthropic Sonnet 4 (brain)
- ElevenLabs (voice)
- Twilio (phone number)
- Deepgram (transcription)
